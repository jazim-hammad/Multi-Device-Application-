Welcome to Chips Types

Chips are a beloved snack food that come in many different shapes, sizes, and flavors. Here are a few fun facts about chips:

  • Chips were invented in 1853 by George Crum, a Native American chef working at a restaurant in New York.
  • The most popular flavor of chips is plain/salted.
  • Potato chips are the most consumed type of chips in the world.

Potato Chips

Thinly sliced, fried or baked potatoes that are often flavored with salt or other seasonings.

Tortilla Chips

A snack food made from corn tortilla that are cut into wedges and then fried or baked until crisp.

Corn Chips

Similar to tortilla chips but usually thicker and crunchier, made from a mixture of cornmeal, water, and salt.

About Chips Types

Chips Types is a website dedicated to exploring the various types of chips available in the world. Whether you're a fan of potato chips, tortilla chips, or corn chips, we've got you covered!
